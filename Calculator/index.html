Welcome to Calculator.io

Need quick calculations? calc.io is here to make your life easier! You can solve basic calculations using this calculator. Simply use the buttons on the right to input numbers and operators, and the result will be displayed instantly.

Features
  • Basic arithmetic: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Backspace button to correct mistakes.
  • Clear button to reset the screen.

  • The first mechanical calculator was invented in 1642 by Blaise Pascal.

  • Modern calculators can perform functions beyond basic math, like trigonometry and logarithms.
